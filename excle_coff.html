Coffee Sales Dashboard

This dashboard analyzes coffee sales from 2019 to 2022, showcasing sales trends across coffee types, roast preferences, and customer loyalty. It provides a comprehensive summary of sales performance by country and customer behavior.

Key Features

  • Date Range Analysis: Examine sales over time using dynamic filters for date ranges.
  • Product Breakdown: Analyze sales based on coffee types (Arabica, Liberica, Robusta, etc.) and roast preferences (Dark, Medium, Light).
  • Geographic Insights: Identify top-performing countries, with the United States leading in revenue generation.
  • Data Model: Created a comprehensive data model integrating multiple datasets for seamless querying and visualization.
  • Customer Insights: Highlight top customers and their purchase behaviors.

    • Tools & Techniques Used

  • Excel Pivot Tables: Organized and summarized raw data for analysis.
  • Data Cleaning: Standardized, filtered, and structured data to ensure accuracy.
  • Excel Dashboarding: Created visually appealing and interactive dashboards using slicers and charts.

Coffee Sales Dashboard, the analysis provided actionable insights into sales performance across regions, customer behavior, and product preferences, helping stakeholders understand trends and identify growth opportunities.
