Pizza Sales Dashboard in Excel

Objective

The primary objective of this project was to analyze sales data for a pizza business to uncover trends, identify top-performing categories, and present actionable insights through a user-friendly dashboard. This analysis aimed to assist the business in making data-driven decisions to optimize operations, improve sales strategies, and enhance overall performance.

Overview

This project involved creating an interactive and visually appealing dashboard to analyze pizza sales data using Microsoft Excel. The goal was to identify key sales trends, performance metrics, and actionable insights to support business decision-making.

Project Highlights

  • Key Metrics Tracked:
    • Total Revenue: $817,860.05
    • Total Orders: 21,350
    • Average Order Value: $38.31
    • Total Pizzas Sold: 49,574
    • Average Pizzas per Order: 2.32
  • Business Insights:
    • Top-performing Days & Times: Highest sales occur on weekends, especially Friday and Saturday evenings, between 12 PM–1 PM and after 4 PM.
    • Best and Worst Sellers:
      • Best Selling Pizzas: Classic Deluxe Pizza, Barbecue Chicken Pizza.
      • Least Popular: The Brie Carre Pizza.
    • Category Performance: The Classic category and large-sized pizzas contribute the most to sales.
    • Hourly Sales Trends: Peak hours are between 12 PM and 8 PM, with a gradual decline afterward.
  • Visualizations:
    • Bar charts to display daily and hourly sales trends.
    • Pie charts for sales distribution by pizza size and category.
    • Ranked lists for the top 5 best and worst-selling pizzas.
    • Category-wise comparison of total pizzas sold.

Tools & Techniques Used

  • Software: Microsoft Excel (Dashboard features and PivotTables).
  • Data Visualization: Custom charts, color-coded themes, and dynamic slicers for interactivity.
  • Data Analysis: Identified patterns using metrics such as sales volume, order frequency, and revenue.

Challenges Addressed

  • Designed an intuitive layout to present complex data clearly.
  • Highlighted critical business patterns to assist in strategic decisions.
  • Improved engagement with vibrant visuals and meaningful segmentation.

Outcome

This dashboard effectively demonstrates how data analysis and visualization can provide valuable business insights. It’s a powerful tool for tracking performance, identifying areas of improvement, and optimizing operational strategies for a pizza business.
