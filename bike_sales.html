Bike Sales Dashboard Excel

This dashboard provides insights into bike sales trends, exploring customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and commuting preferences. It highlights key patterns based on income, education, and region.

Key Features

  • Demographic Analysis: Examine trends by marital status, education level, and gender.
  • Age Group Trends: Detailed analysis of bike purchases segmented by age brackets (Adolescent, Middle-aged, Old).
  • Commute Preferences: Track bike purchases across varying commute distances.
  • Regional Breakdown: Compare sales across Europe, North America, and the Pacific regions.

    • Tools & Techniques Used

  • Excel Pivot Tables: Summarized large datasets to uncover trends
  • Data Cleaning: Standardized, filtered, and structured data to ensure accuracy.
  • Excel Dashboarding: Designed interactive dashboards with charts and slicers for a user-friendly experience

The Bike Sales Dashboard effectively explored demographic and geographic factors influencing bike purchases, offering valuable perspectives for targeted marketing strategies and customer engagement improvements.
